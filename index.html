What is Bayern München?

As is well known, FC Bayern is a germane soccer club from Munich City and currently one of the best clubs in the world. Nowadays the club participates in the Bundesligue. Its nickname is the Giant of Bawiera. The stadium is called Allianz Arena located in Werner Heisenberg Munich, Germany. Unfortunately many people put it below teams like Real Madrid or Barcelona, but FC Bayern reached its greatness, with effort, since its foundation lived very complicated moments is why to strengthen and understand the philosophy of the club is necessary to know about its beginnings.

Bayern Beginnings

The Club began in February 1900, when Franz John, the first president of the club, together with 17 members, decided to sign a document, which certified the creation of FC Bayern Sports Club. This club was founded to assume the sporting leadership in Munich, and the first players were part of the members who signed the certification document. The first colors established were red and white, Bayern got the Leopoldstraée stadium as its first place where could be played the local matches.

The Important Achievements.

In 1932 the club achieves the first national title. Then, in 1944 Bayern got its first championship league. After thirteen years, in 1957, the team conquest the German cup. Further in 1965 upgrade to the Bundesligue, as a consequence, one year later the Bayern won the German Cup and in addition, after three years got its first doublet. The doublet consists of the cup and the bundesligue titles. Furthermore in 1974 the team won the Europe Champions Cup gained by the first time. In 1975 they made their mark at European level after winning the European Cup, and the next year, the club won the Intercontinental Cup as well. (0-0 and 2-0, against Belo Horizonte). These were Bayern's last international titles during the 20th century. 4 At the start of the 21st century. they achieved six national championships, including five doublets which filled the trophy cabinet of FC Bayern. The most dramatic victory the club experienced was in the 2001 the team got the Champions League. In 2002 the club became a joint- stock company, which resulted in the construction of the Allianz Arena, Stadium, and a renovation of the club's offices On the other hand in, 2012/2013 season, Firstly FC Bayern won the Super Cup. Next the team could get a historic treble when obtained a Bundesligue, German Cup and The Champions League. Over the next few years, FC Bayern continued to dominate the competitions in Germany and today, they have won seven Bundesligue titles in a row and 4 German Cups.

The reasons why is the best club.

Firstly, the team has gained success since 1970 decade unquestionably as the highest champion of Germany and internationally as one of the biggest in Europe. As a result of tireless work, great desire, ambitious and team spirit, played and lived by the players, who have used the team uniform. FC Bayern has faced through some very difficult and hard moments since its foundation. Especially when the team was marginalized, discriminated, and lost many members along the difficult situations on account, World War II. After all, the club did not give up. Otherwise, the team rise up and position itself as the best team in German football, doing it by the way that they already know, "winning" and fighting until the end. Moreover of the things Bayern are most admired , compared to other clubs, is the way in which the directors manage the club, as they do not spend the money, in large amounts, but trust the young talents, and in signing players, with the club philosophy. The team is the fifth most popular football club in Europe with 20.7 million supporters, and the most popular football club in Germany with 10 million supporters. Bayern represent its economic power, generating profits of 289.5 million, making it the most successful sports team brands. For UEFA, Bayern's economic model is the most acclaimed.

Bayern as a role model

FC Bayern has been characterized by its German phrase "Mia san Mia", translated in English as "We are us", which represents the pride and essence of the club, as stated above, team was despised and victim of many injustices due to, the Second World War, that is why his phrase represents an identity to continue acting with the head high and achieve your goals. Nevertheless, in certain occasions the Club comes to be hated, in different parts of Germany, even so Bayern manage a philosophy that the team does not look for to be of the pleasure of all, the team, simply wants to win all. Finally the club is an example to follow because even though in later years Bayern was not accepted in the Bundesliga, suffered discrimination and lost a lot of members, the team was able to overcome and work hard to achieve all that it has achieved today, the team has not been given anything, that is why historically the club shows a great life lesson that we should all have which is not to stop fighting until the end to reach the top of success.
